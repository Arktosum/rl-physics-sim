rl-physics-sim

Teaching agents to balance a pendulum, four different ways

Every demo below trains live in your browser, on a dedicated Worker thread — nothing is pre-recorded. Four reinforcement-learning approaches were tried in order, each one picking up where the last hit a real ceiling: tabular Q-learning → DQN → REINFORCE → PPO.

The physics engine is built from scratch — no physics library. Points integrate with Verlet integration (position history instead of explicit velocity), and rods between them are solved as distance constraints via Position-Based Dynamics, the same family of technique used in cloth and rope simulations. Nothing here comes from a physics engine or an RL library — both halves are hand-built.
